Easy Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, garlic and ginger, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • half cup coconut milk

Steps:

  1. Step 1 - Ingredients preparation
  2. Seal chicken in pan with a little oil
  3. Add in chopped onion, garlic and ginger
    1. Cook for 5 minutes to soften
    2. Add in curry powder and cinnamon
      • Stir and cook for a further minute
  4. Add in tomato paste and coconut milk
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes - Until the chicken is cooked through
